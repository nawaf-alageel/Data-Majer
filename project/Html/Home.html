What is Data Science?

Data Science is the study of using data to find answers and solve problems. It combines skills from areas like math, computer programming, and statistics to analyze large amounts of data, find patterns, and make useful predictions. Data Science plays a crucial role in many fields, such as healthcare, business, and technology, helping organizations make smarter decisions. Whether it's predicting trends, improving products, or solving everyday problems, Data Science shapes how we understand and use information.

Why Study Data Science?

Data Science is one of the most in-demand fields today, with applications in almost every industry. As organizations continue to generate more data, they need skilled professionals who can turn that data into valuable insights.
