NEC Cooperative Training Program

Link: https://nec.gov.sa...

About the Program: The Cooperative Training Program serves as a solid bridge connecting academic learning and the job market. It is one of the most prestigious educational initiatives that integrates theory with practice. The National Events Center aims to provide students with valuable opportunities to gain the best experience, enabling them to apply their academic knowledge in real-world work environments. It is a true investment in the students' future, where they receive guidance from experts, face real professional challenges, and prepare effectively for their careers. Students become more efficient, confident, and ready to join the job market and contribute effectively in their fields of specialization.

Majors: Business Administration, Accounting, Finance, Human Resources, IT, Information Systems, Cybersecurity, Industrial Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Computer Engineering, Law, Event Management, Supply Chain, Risk Management

Eligibility: Saudi nationality, undergraduate students, passing the personal interview, proficiency in English

SNB Cooperative Training Program

Link: https://www.alahli.com...

Eligibility: Saudi nationality, an active university student (minimum bachelor's degree) from a Ministry of Education accredited university or institution, the training is part of graduation requirements, GPA of no less than 3.75/5 or 2.75/4, passing tests, interviews, and evaluation processes.

Majors: Business Administration, Technology, Engineering (Industrial Engineering, Computer Science, Software), Legal Studies

Required Documents: CV, National ID copy, University approval letter stating that cooperative training is a graduation requirement, University approval letter specifying the required training period and contact details of the training supervisor.
